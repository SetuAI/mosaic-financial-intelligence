MOSAIC is a production-grade multi-agent financial intelligence system that:

* Ingests SEC filings automatically from EDGAR — 121 documents across 6 tech companies
* Indexes 5,617 semantic passages for millisecond retrieval
* Runs 5 specialised AI agents that detect signals impossible to generate with a single prompt
* Routes high-stakes signals through a human review gate before publishing
* Composes professional intelligence briefs that read like analyst research
* Passes a 5-case evaluation suite with no regressions
* Deploys to AWS with a single command

The full pipeline runs in under 5 minutes and costs roughly $0.30 in API calls. A human analyst team doing equivalent coverage would take 2–3 days per cycle.
